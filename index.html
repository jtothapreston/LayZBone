The Cabin

On the Sierra doorstep, off the beaten path.

Welcome to Lay-Z Bone Lodge — a beautiful chalet-style cabin in the heart of Big Trees Village, where vintage camp vibes meet modern amenities. Recently remodeled, spacious, and designed for groups both large and small.

An open floor plan flows from the fully equipped kitchen to a cozy living area anchored by a wood-burning fireplace. A secret kids loft tucks away for little adventurers, while the separate extra-large game room keeps everyone entertained — rain, snow, or shine.

Explore the Area

Adventures to be had.

Lay-Z Bone Lodge puts you within reach of some of Northern California's most spectacular outdoor experiences — in every season.

21 miles away
Bear Valley Ski Resort
Hit the slopes in winter — skiing, snowboarding, and lessons for all levels in the high Sierra.
24 miles away
Lake Alpine
Crystal-clear alpine waters for fishing, kayaking, paddleboarding, and hiking the surrounding peaks.
23 miles away
Moaning Caverns
Explore one of California's largest caverns — zip down on a rappel adventure or take a guided walking tour.
25 miles away
Natural Bridges
Hike to stunning rock arch formations carved by the Stanislaus River — a hidden Sierra gem.
18 miles away
Murphys Wine Country
Discover award-winning tasting rooms along the Gold Rush wine trail, including Ironstone Winery.
Minutes Away
Big Trees Rec Center
Two pools, tennis courts, bocce, disc golf, basketball, and a playground — all included with your stay.
Minutes Away
The Lube Room
An old western bar with serious character — cold drinks, great burgers, and live music. The perfect spot to unwind after a day on the slopes or the trails.
Minutes Away
Big Trees State Park
Stand among two ancient groves of giant sequoias — among the tallest trees on the planet. Hike the North and South Grove trails, fish the Stanislaus River, and discover why these trees have awed visitors since 1852.
